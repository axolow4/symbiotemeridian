[[Humans]] or other sentient beings (inc. [[Divergent life forms|divergent life]]) that underwent a [[Devil's Pact, Conversion|Devil's Pact of Conversion]], having all of its matter tinged with spinning [[Drive]]. The matter does not become [[Drive]], but always will have a unique (but similar to the original [[Devils|Devil]]'s) signature of [[Drive]] even in external [[Drive]] usage (i.e. [[Flowing Drive]] or [[Pulse Drive]]). Through a link to the original [[Devils|Devil]], the matter of the [[Demons]] will always emit [[Drive]], too. 
In the [[Devil's Pact, Conversion|Pact]], the devotee gives up a possession in order to: 
	1: Activate their specific technique if not already activated
	2: Gain a new technique by force if they already possess one
	3: Open up [[Drive]] pathways in the body if not already opened
	4: Gain sufficient spin in a suitable organ's cells' particles to hold [[Drive]] if not sufficient
	5: Gain sufficient [[Drive]] to fill up their organ's [[Drive]] stores
[[Demons]] won't be bound to only use their specific technique for use cases and objectives dicted by their [[Devils|Devil]], similar to servants of [[Angels]]. However, the bulk of the [[Drive]] supplied to the technique must be from their own stores; use of the technique will not make the 's matter emit more of the [[Devils]]'s [[Drive]] so while it can contribute, they must, for the most part, use their technique of their own effort.
