Hyper accelerated evolution, hastened by the purposeful ebb and flow of [[Drive]] from [[Drive consciousnesses]]. These life forms come in species of single-digit populations, with no ability to reproduce, and no genetic information. Rather than come into being through iterations of life, undergoing symbiotic relationships between organelles at the cellular level, forming large organisms, the tissues of these life forms were constructed immediately, from scratch, through the movement of molecules powered by [[Drive]]. Without genetic information, [[Drive]] sustains these organisms to random degrees, some living for minutes, and some alive since their birth up to 4 billion years ago.
