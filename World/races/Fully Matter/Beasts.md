Animals, in-universe indistinguishable from animals that already exist in real life, but still mentioned for the sake of referral to fictional life
Usually referring to larger, possible grotesque, possibly elegant fauna, for the simple reason most of the fictional life will be that, but also can include smaller beings, like fairies, pixies, Pokemon/Studio Ghibli/ATLAesque animal hybrid things
As normal animals, Beasts won't usually have access to [[Drive]] but some species may live with it ingrained much like the [[Kichiku]] and silicon races
