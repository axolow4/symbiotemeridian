The exact same humans as in real life, barring [[Drive]]-related differences. Remarkably unremarkable, majority indifferent to the world of conflict beneath their vision. Exceptionally good at being unexceptional, with the exception of producing an exceptional candidate for exceptional restoration, exceptional pacts or, in exceptional cases, exceptional use of unrefined [[Drive]]. Niche research on [[Drive]] as an unknown form of energy is conducted, unnoticed by the public, and technologies taking advantage of [[Pulse Drive|pulse Drive]] slowly develop.
