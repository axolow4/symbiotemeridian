Similar to [[Metaphysical entities|metaphysical entities]], but of [[Drive]]. These are sort of hiveminds, shards of consciousness of the entire [[Drive]] pool of the universe. These shards of consciousness innately lack the ability to communicate, so by [[Drive]] hosts using the [[Drive]] and communicating, fighting, procreating, et cetera, the shards can connect, and thus [[Drive]] consciousnesses are formed. Once made, [[Drive]] consciousnesses can never denature unless under severe, intense conditions.
