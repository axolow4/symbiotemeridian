Beings made of solid, spinning [[Drive]]. The [[Drive]] of Devils is in a random structure and has an identical resonant frequency to [[Angels]], halting the particles of [[Drive]] in the random structure the more the [[Resonant Flux]] increases. Devils, contrary to popular [[Humans]] belief and culture, are not inherently evil, nor do they oppose [[Angels]]. Devils live for similar purposes to [[Angels]], though they may use roundabout or detour methods to fulfil their goals.
Devils are the main inhabitants of [[Hell]], much like [[Humans]] are to our Solar System.
