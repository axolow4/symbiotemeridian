In the [[Devil's Pact, Conversion|Pact]], the devotee gives up a possession in order to: 
	1: Activate their specific technique if not already activated
	2: Possibly gain a new technique by force if they already possess one
	3: Open up [[Drive]] pathways in the body if not already opened
	4: Gain sufficient spin in a suitable organ's cells' particles to hold [[Drive]] if not sufficient
	5: Gain sufficient [[Drive]] to fill up their organ's [[Drive]] stores

If the newly formed [[Demons|Demon]] hasn't activated the technique appended to their genome, the Pact conscripts the genetic information regarding it and the organ for the specific technique is instantly created within the [[Demons]]. However, if the [[Demons]] already has their original technique activated with an intact, functional organ, the [[Devils]] may redifferentiate some of the [[Demons]]'s flesh into another organ to accommodate an additional technique.

If the newly formed [[Demons|Demon]] does not have a [[Drive]] system, the [[Devils]] will construct an unopened one for the [[Demons]]. The Pact will open the newly constructed [[Flux]] system. Similarly, if the [[Flux]] system is present but opened, the [[Devils]] takes no action and the Pact opens the existing [[Flux]] system. 

If the particles of the relevant [[Demons|Demon]]'s organ do not possess enough spin or any at all, the Pact will give them enough to hold considerable [[Drive]], enough for an amount of [[Drive]] dependent on the value of the possession that was given up. 

Finally, if the [[Drive]] present in the [[Demons|Demon]]'s stores is insufficient (which it must be if spin was granted to the [[Demons]] for higher capacity) then the Pact will grant them sufficient [[Drive]] that is separate from the [[Devils]]'s [[Drive]] and fully theirs.

The [[Demons]]'s instated technique, if additional and not the one from their genome, is a capillary technique taken from the relevant [[Devils]]. The [[Devils]] loses this technique, and regenerates a new one in its place. As part of the Pact, the [[Demons]]'s particles also emit the [[Devils]]'s own [[Drive]], though not enough to sustain the technique instated.
