In this Pact, a possession is given up by a sentient being, though one lesser in value to a possession that would be given up for a [[Devils]]'s Pact of [[Devil's Pact, Conversion]]. In this case, the devotee remains their original race, as they do not constantly receive [[Drive]] of the relevant [[Devils]]. The devotee, however, gains a permanent increase in particle spin from the Pact, and may either gain a new technique/activate an existing, inert technique, gain considerably more [[Drive]], or bestow a whole signature (or a number of traits) onto their [[Drive]].
